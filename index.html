About Us

Several years ago when Traci was about to graduate high school it seemed the world was wide open for all kinds of ventures and adventures. Against the advice of several friends Traci chose nursing as her career. With an on-the-ready broad and sincere smile she knew there was a lot to offer in helping the aging loved ones. Now with broad and excellent experience in caring for seniors both in hospital settings and in facilities she has her little lovely place where residents are simply...family.

Swiss haus was started to satisfy the needs of families and their loved seniors. Round the clock dedicated staff who are licensed and skilled are augmented by nurse Traci R.N. and all needed medical expertise including a round-the-clock house-call MD. Daily exercises, activities and entertaining in beautiful and uplifting surroundings.

Residents walk up and down our private road, enjoy landscaped grounds with live freindly chickens and vegetable gardens. Light-filled rooms -decorated by each resident- are equipped with sattelite connected flat-screen TVs. Brain-stimulating activities and fitness sessions are conducted daily around freshly prepared meals and snacks. A video autobiogrophy production is facilitated by staff to share with family clips in memoris and life events.

Services

Everyday Living

partial or complete assistance with all the basic services including meal and snack preparation and feeding. toileting, bathing and grooming. laundry and housekeeping chores

Living Well

in-haus hair styling, manicure, pedicure and foot massage

Life Enrichment

daily newspaper readings, magazines and books including library-on-wheels. puzzles and linguistic exercises. weekly activities conducted in-haus by a professional. professionally run painting classes. professional piano and live entertainment.

Feeling Well

24/7 optional haus-call MD, RN medical case management coordinated with family. coordinating all needed medical providers including pharmacies, speach, occupational and physical therapists.

Staying Connected

haus phone is available to all for private incoming and outgoing calls. email and internet browsing is available on haus tablet with free WiFi internet in all rooms. in-room Skype calling

Video Storybook

staff-facilitated video recording of video clips recalling life events. clips are edited by residents, uploaded to a private website and arranged later to form a documentary-like production. residents decide whom to share the clips with.
