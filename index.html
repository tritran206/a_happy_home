What is an Adult Family Home?

“An Adult Family Home is one of the many options in our area that is able to provide hands-on care to older adults. An AFH can provide care for a maximum of six clients and is a great option for someone who requires more one-on-one attention because of the lower resident-to-caregiver ratio.”

Our Mission

To maintain and encourage a healthy, safe and fulfilling care, with quality care provided by:

  • Registered nurse
  • House-call physician
  • Experienced state certified staff
  • State licensed hair stylist, licensed music therapy
  • Artists, musicians, entertainers, etc.
  • Books, magazines, videos.

To engage everyone to their fullest comfortable capacity in activities that:

  • Give them a reason to get up in the morning
  • Enrich their lives and their families
  • Fill their day with joy
  • Enhance physical ability and fitness
  • Stimulate mental sharpness
  • Connect with life events

A Happy Home is a family owned business. Our mission is to promote the health and well-being of our residents by providing high quality care and encourage meaningful daily living. Our staff is certified in the fundamentals of caregiving, first aid, and CPR. They are well trained in dementia and mental health care.

A Happy Home was started to satisfy the needs of families and their loved seniors. Round the clock dedicated staff who are licensed and skilled are augmented by nurse Traci R.N. and all needed medical expertise including a round-the-clock house-call MD. Daily exercises, activities and entertaining in beautiful and uplifting surroundings.

Meet the Staff

Traci Tran

In-home Nurse, RN, BSN

Traci obtained her nursing degree at Shoreline Community College and received her BSN at the University of Washington. She has been a nurse for almost 10 years. She began her career in health care in high school by working as a certified nursing assistant at nursing homes and group homes. She also worked at Swedish Medical Center and Kindred hospitals in acute care settings. She currently owns a NW Home Care Consultant business where she provides nursing delegation to multiple adult family homes in Snohomish and King county. Her passion for caring for the vulnerable adults has inspired her to open her own an adult family home.

Valeri Tran

Provider

Valeri acquired her degree in Noninvasive Cardiovascular Technology at Spokane community College. She has worked at Evergreen Hospital and Tacoma General Hospital as an Echocardiographer in an inpatient/outpatient care setting for over 5 years. In addition, she was a resident manager at Swiss Haus AFH caring for residents with various progressive diseases. She is HCA (Home Care Aid) certified with vast experiences in patient care and management. Her skillset and experiences has translated well to the Adult family home setting. With A Happy Home AFH, Valeri is able to be an advocate for her residents and assist them on living their life to the fullest extent of their ability.

Our Services

Wellness

24/7 optional house-call MD, RN medical case management coordinated with family. Coordinating all needed medical providers including pharmacies, speech, occupational and physical therapists.

Everyday Living

Ranging from independent to complete assistance with all the basic services including meal preparation, feeding, toileting, bathing and grooming, laundry and housekeeping chores.

Life Enrichment

Music therapy, daily newspaper readings, magazines and books including library-on-wheels. Puzzles and linguistic exercises. Weekly activities conducted in-house by a professional. Painting classes with an art teacher. Professional piano and live entertainment.

Living Well (optional)

in-haus hair styling, manicure, pedicure and foot massage.

Staying Connected

A phone is available to all for private incoming and outgoing calls. Email and internet browsing is available on haus tablet with free Wi-Fi internet in all rooms. In-room Skype calling is also available upon request.
